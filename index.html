What is the churn rate calculator?
The churn rate calculator is a tool that will give you additional insights about your clientele by measuring the percentage of them lost at the end of a period.
How to calculate churn rate?
To calculate churn rate you should use the following formula:
churn rate = (customers lost during a period / costumers at start of a period) * 100 Why use churn rate calculator?
The churn rate calculator is used in order to calculate the percentage of the clinets lost at the end of a period.
